Forecast Channel Recreation Features



  • Check the weather in your area with geolocation support! You'll have to modify this yourself with your own city if using a Wii U or 3DS...
  • Choose between the °F and °C temperature unit.
  • Download and spin the Globe as much as you want! No, you can't check the weather on it, nice try.
  • In the top left corner on the pages, you can see a clock that was used in some versions of the channel.
  • Listen to the short but beautiful collection of music the channel used.
  • Read the manual that was accessible on the channel! This might not be useful but it's just for your entertainment.
  • That cursor used on most Wii games/apps/etc. is used on the pages! This is only visible on your computer, though...
  • Use the Wii NTLG PGothic font, the same font used to display temperature, current weather, etc. on the channel.
  • View the UV Index. We're lazy so we put a widget on there. :P
