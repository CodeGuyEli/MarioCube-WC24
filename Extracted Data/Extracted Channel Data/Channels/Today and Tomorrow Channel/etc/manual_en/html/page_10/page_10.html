Care Hints

This feature gives you hints on how to take care of yourself and your living environment today or tomorrow. Keeping clean and tidy can keep your mind fresh, so why not check out your care hints?

Note: Care hints can be displayed for a single Mii or for up to 6 Mii characters together.

  1. Select the Care Icon

    Select from the Main Screen (see “5. Viewing the Main Screen”).

  1. Choose Mii Character(s)

    Drag the Mii character or characters for whom you want to get care hints into the area. To drag a Mii, point at it and press and hold and together. Drag Mii characters out of the area and back onto the lucky wheel to remove them. Select OK to confirm once you’re happy with the combination of Mii characters.

Note: You can also drag a Mii directly onto the icon on the Main Screen to start adding Mii characters whose care hints you want to check.
  1. Show Lucky Care Hints

    Once you select OK on the previous screen, a short animation sequence will be shown, after which three lucky care hints will be displayed.

    Note: You can skip the animation and proceed directly to the hints by selecting the icon.

When you have read the hints, select FRESH! to return to the Main Screen.
